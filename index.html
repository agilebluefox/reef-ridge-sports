Getting Started

Get started scuba diving with the PADI Open Water Diver certification. You start with the e-learning kit, then follow up with three (3) pool training sessions and complete the certification with four (4) open water dives. Cost: $500

PADI E-Learning Kit

With this convenient online option you can start learning right away and proceed at your own pace. This course provides you with the background knowledge you'll need to dive safely. Stop by our shop to get started today!

Pool Training Sessions

These three (3) sessions take place in a local aquatic facility and can be started as you're wrapping up with the online course. You'll need to have a snorkel, mask, and fins (you can rent items if necessary for an additional fee) - we'll provide all of the scuba equipment.

Open Water Training Dives

The final component required for the completion of your Open Water Diver certification involves four (4) open water training dives. These dives are completed at a local quarry over a two-day period.
